Tarang Joshi

Photographer · Visual Storyteller

Tarang Joshi is a visual artist and documentary photographer whose work explores the delicate tension between the natural world and human intervention. His photographic practice is rooted in long‑form observation, capturing landscapes that hover between stillness and transformation.

His photo books Ungreen Scape and Between Shadows — published under the imprint 14Trees — are physical artifacts of that vision, merging fine‑art printing with immersive storytelling. Each book invites the viewer to step into a quiet, contemplative space where light, form, and texture speak louder than words.

Beyond his personal work, Tarang is the founder of EkDharani, a community‑driven initiative that channels proceeds from book sales into social causes. He believes that art can be a gentle but powerful force for change.
